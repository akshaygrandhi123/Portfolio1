Publications

AidBot: A ChatGPT based Chatbot for e-Commerce Platforms

Conference: 8th IET Smart Cities Symposium (SCS 2024) | Date: December 2024

Publisher: IET | Date: April 2025

This research introduces AidBot, an AI-powered customer service chatbot designed for e-commerce platforms, leveraging ChatGPT and prompt engineering techniques. AidBot offers dynamic, human-like responses and supports multilingual interactions using a fine-tuned GPT model integrated with OpenAI APIs. The chatbot is capable of handling various customer service scenarios—from order tracking to issue resolution—while maintaining a conversational, user-friendly interface. AidBot also features secure logging of conversation data and is tested across diverse use cases, including typos and grammatical errors, showcasing its robustness and adaptability in real-world applications.
